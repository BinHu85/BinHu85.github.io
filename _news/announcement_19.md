:star2: Our project, "Heterogeneous Multi-Robot Waste Detection with Conformal Runtime Monitoring," has been selected for the **NVIDIA Academic Grant Program**! Through this grant, NVIDIA is donating **4x RTX PRO 6000 Blackwell Max-Q Workstation Edition GPUs** and **2x Jetson AGX Orin Dev Kits** to the University of Houston in support of our research on trustworthy, efficient, and deployable multi-robot perception systems for waste detection and monitoring. We are grateful to **NVIDIA** for their generous support of academic research and innovation.
